The Origin

WHERE IT STARTED

GeoDMus didn't launch from a pitch deck. It started with a simple, real problem — businesses around me needed marketing and had nowhere to turn. Friends with ideas, family with businesses, people working hard but invisible online.

The seed was planted inside a tax office — Premier Tax — where Giovanni first got his hands on real marketing for real businesses. That office became the first chapter and gave birth to Premier Group — one of seven ventures now operating under the GeoDMus ecosystem.

Before that, there was Bass Consulting — a corporate credit and financing startup where Giovanni sharpened his instincts for business infrastructure. The company wound down, but the lessons never did. Every failure is a blueprint for what comes next.

The Founder

THE MAN BEHIND IT

Born in Bell Gardens, South LA in 1985. Raised between Los Angeles, Chicago, and Sahuayo, Mexico. That upbringing didn't just shape his language skills — it shaped how he sees the world.

Bilingual, bicultural, and built for it. Giovanni's fluency in English and Spanish isn't just a skill — it's a bridge to communities that are often underserved in the digital economy.

His love for technology started young — watching Terminator, War Games, and Star Wars — asking the question every builder eventually asks: what if I could build that?

Life hasn't been a straight line. There were real setbacks. Losing everything. Starting over. Being humbled at an age when most people expect to already have it figured out. But those moments didn't break him. They built him.

Today he's building not just an agency but an ecosystem — seven ventures, one vision, and a mission to give every business the digital tools to compete and win.
