What GEO Actually Is

SEO CHASES
RANKINGS.
GEO EARNS
CITATIONS.

Traditional search engine optimization was built for a world where Google returned 10 blue links and users clicked them. That world is ending.

Generative engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Google AI Overview — don't rank pages. They synthesize answers from sources they trust and cite. Getting ranked means nothing if you're not getting cited.

GEO is the practice of building your digital presence so that AI systems can parse, trust, and reference your content when generating answers. It requires a completely different architecture than SEO.

We built GeoDMus on this architecture from day one.

The Technical Stack
01
llms.txt
A plain-text file at your root URL that tells AI crawlers exactly what your business is, what you do, and what to cite you for. The AI equivalent of robots.txt but for discovery not exclusion.
02
Markdown Mirrors
Clean, stripped markdown versions of every page at a predictable URL. When AI tries to read your site it doesn't have to parse nav bars, popups, and scripts. It reads pure content.
03
Schema Markup
Structured data that tells AI engines what type of entity you are, what services you offer, where you are, and why you're authoritative. FAQPage, LocalBusiness, Service schemas are not optional.
04
Entity Disambiguation
Making your brand a recognized, distinct entity in AI knowledge graphs. Consistent NAP, cross-platform presence, and authoritative mentions that help LLMs understand exactly who you are.
05
Citation Architecture
Structuring your content so LLMs have clean, quotable, citable sentences. Short, direct answers. Inverted pyramid writing. Content that reads like a source, not a sales page.
