Pricing FAQ

QUICK ANSWERS

Can I really get a discount if I can't afford the standard price?

Yes — and we mean it. We built this agency to serve communities. If your revenue doesn't support $799/mo yet, we have a conversation in your discovery call and build a plan together. Results trigger the upgrade, not a fixed date.

What's the minimum commitment?

3 months on all plans including custom entry plans. After that, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No penalties, no lock-in games. We want clients who stay because we're delivering — not because they're trapped.

Do I own everything you build for me?

100%. Your website files, your domain, your social accounts, your email list — all yours from day one. If you ever leave, we hand everything over cleanly. Your assets are never held hostage.

Can I upgrade my plan as my business grows?

That's the whole point. Most clients start on Launchpad and grow into Growth Engine as results compound. Upgrades happen at any billing cycle — no waiting, no penalty, just more firepower when you're ready.

Are there setup fees or hidden costs?

No setup fees. No hidden costs. What you agree to is what you pay. Third-party tools like ad spend or domain registration are always disclosed upfront — and you own those accounts directly.

How do payment plans work for one-time projects?

One-time projects can be split into two payments — 50% to start, 50% on delivery. For larger builds we can discuss a 3-payment structure. We figure it out in the discovery call.
