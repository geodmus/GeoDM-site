A Note From the Founder

I've been on-chain since before 2015. I'm a Bitcoin maximalist — I believe in sound money, self-custody, and the foundational power of the original chain. But I'm not blind to what's being built everywhere else. Ethereum laid the infrastructure for programmable money. Solana proved speed and scale are possible on-chain. BNB brought DeFi to a global audience who couldn't afford ETH gas fees.

Real estate will be tokenized. Business ownership will live on-chain. Decentralized markets will lend, borrow, and exchange digital assets without a middleman. This isn't coming. It's already here — and the businesses that understand it now will have an enormous head start.

When GeoDMus builds Web3 for your business, you're not getting a checkbox on a services list. You're getting a decade of conviction and real on-chain experience.
