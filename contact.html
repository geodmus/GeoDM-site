Quick Answers

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How fast can you launch my website?
Most sites go live within 7–14 days of our kickoff call. Complex platforms take 3–4 weeks. We move fast without cutting corners.
Do you require a long-term contract?
No. All retainers run month-to-month after an initial 3-month commitment. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
Can I pay with crypto?
Yes — we accept Bitcoin, ETH, and USDC via Coinbase Commerce. We also accept credit/debit via Stripe and ACH bank transfer.
Do you work with businesses outside Chicago?
Absolutely. We serve clients nationwide. Chicago is home base but we've worked with businesses across the US and internationally.
What if I just need one thing, like a website?
We can do one-off projects. Send us a message and we'll put together a custom quote. The retainer model is for ongoing growth partnerships.
I'm not tech-savvy. Will I be able to manage my site?
Yes. We build for ease of use and provide full training. And if you ever need help, we're a phone call away. That's what the retainer is for.
